ABOUT US

Running a
successful business
since 1955

We, “R.P. Kaushal Steels”, are one of the reputed Wholesale Supplier, Wholesaler, Trader, Exporter & Importer of steel products. Our products are highly demanded by our clients owing to their characteristics like durability, sturdy construction, dimensional accuracy and high tensile strength to name a few. These features are found in our products because of the fact that we use only good quality raw material while producing them.

Our sound infrastructure and our team of experts have together been a major aid to us in keeping our clients satisfied with our products. It is due to the efforts channelized by our learned professionals that we have been successful in developing a large product line. Our customized solutions and a large production capacity have also helped us gain the trust of our clients in the domestic market.

Our Services

Cutting

Material can be cut to your required lengths. Production cutting is available on large quantity orders. Our standard cutting tolerance is 1 inch or 25mm for all items. Special cutting tolerances are available upon request.

Fast Delivery

We are follow F.O.R (free on road) for large quantity orders. Special orders can be ship with in 1-2 days. We provide regular lorry service for delivery of your products. Special vehicle can be supply for special orders.

Non-Stock Procurement

R.P. Kaushal Steels Company is continuously adding additional material types and sizes to our inventory. We also offer our experienced sales team's ability to obtain non-stock items to fill your order, because we strive to fulfil all of your material needs.
